How 17-0 Works

17-0 is a historical football roster simulator. You do not play games manually. You draft a small roster, then the app uses player and unit ratings to estimate a full 17-game season.

  • Draft slots: QB, RB, WR1, WR2, DEF.
  • Each roll produces a historical franchise pick.
  • You get one re-roll for the entire draft board.
  • After the roster is locked, the simulator calculates your record.

The perfect 17-0 season is intentionally rare. A stacked roster can still land at 15-2 or 16-1 if the balance is not right.
